2012 Texas State Water Plan

Texas Water Development Board

This application graphically displays water planning information from the 2012 State Water Plan. The mapped location of a water provider is keyed to the primary location of that provider; therefore, a water provider with a large service area may be displayed outside the specific area being queried.

For additional information see the 2012 State Water Plan or the 2011 Regional Water Plans.
